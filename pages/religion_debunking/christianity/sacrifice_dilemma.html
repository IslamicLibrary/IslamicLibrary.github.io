The sacrifice dilemma

Credit to Asadullah Ali Al Andalusi The Sacrifice Dilemma Premise 1: God is all perfect which includes the inability to change Premise 2: God sacrificed his son Jesus Premise 3: Sacrifice means to lose something Premise 4: A loss implies a change Conclusion: Therefore God is not perfect since he changed, thus Christianity is false
