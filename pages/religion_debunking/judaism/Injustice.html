Injustice towards prophets in Judaism

The Rambam lays out criteria for a prophet 👇 “Prophecy is bestowed only upon a very wise sage of a strong character, who is never overcome by his natural inclinations in any regard. Instead, with his mind, he overcomes his natural inclinations at all times. He must [also] possess a broad and correct perspective. A person who is full of all these qualities and is physically sound [is fit for prophecy].” Source: "Guide for the Perplexed", Vol. ii, Chapters 36-46.
