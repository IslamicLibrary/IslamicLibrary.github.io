Prophets vs Imams

One objection that rafidahs bring up is that imams are better then the prophets. But in here I will just show the ijma of the scholars of Ahlul sunnah then objections for Shias Imam al-Nawawi (RA) stated: The Status of the Awliya cannot reach the Rank of the Ambiya. Ijma has been decided on this matter. Imam ibn Taymiyyah (RA) stated: The Salaf of this Ummah and the Imams and all the Awliya are in agreement that Prophets are superior to Awliya.
