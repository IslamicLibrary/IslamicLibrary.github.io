Breaking Sawm

When is breaking ur sawm? Sheikh Ibn Kather said in his tasfir of Al-Baqarah Ayah 187 “then complete your Sawm till the nightfall ”means that breaking the fast at the moment of sunset) > Imam an-Nawawi said: Maghrib comes immediately after the sun has set, so that means we break fast when the sun sets.> ‎ثُمَّ أَتِمُّوا الصِّيَامَ إِلَى اللَّيْلِ complete your Sawm when the nightfall
