Aisha is in Paradise

Prophet Muhammed said : "Would you be pleased to be my wife in this worldly life and the hereafter?" I (aisha) said: "By Allah yes" He (Prophet Muhammed) said: "You are my wife in this worldly life and in the hereafter" Sahih Translation: "A’isha (رضي الله عنها) my wife (the wife of prophet Muhammed ﷺ) in Jannah"
