Sun orbits earth

The sun and earth orbit each other, or more precisely, they both orbit around their combined center-of-gravity, a point called a Barycenter - Wikipedia. The barycenter for the sun/earth system is very close to the center of mass of the sun itself, so the sun’s orbital period around it is barely perceptible. The sun is ALSO orbiting the barycenter of every other object that orbits it (all the other planets, asteroids, comets, dust specks, etc).
